338 Acts of Change: Empowering Sri Lanka’s Future Through Data

→ SEE ALL PROJECTS

Sri Lanka's development story involves resilience, growth, and transformation. 338 Acts of Change isn't just a collection of numbers; it reflects a nation's journey, showcasing the impactful work in various communities to shape a better future. Learn more about UN INFO Platform – United Nations in Sri Lanka (2024).

At the heart of this project is a visual chart that breaks down 338 initiatives—each representing a tangible effort to uplift communities, strengthen policy, and build resilience. UNDP Sri Lanka has been a key partner in enabling these transformations.

These 338 dots in the chart are more than just data points; they represent real people, real changes, and real progress. It’s about weaving each initiative into the larger fabric of Sri Lanka’s future.
