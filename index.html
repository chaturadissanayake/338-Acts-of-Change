338 Acts of Change

Empowering Sri Lanka’s Future Through Data

Driving Transformation Through Data

338 Acts of Change highlights a nation’s journey of growth and resilience. Each dot in this visualization represents a real initiative that impacts lives, builds communities, and redefines Sri Lanka’s development.

These initiatives span critical areas like health, education, climate, and governance. The UN INFO Platform is the primary source behind these insights.

Why 338 Matters

Each initiative connects to broader development goals, from grassroots action to policy reform. UNDP Sri Lanka plays a crucial role in enabling these changes.

Tools Behind the Story

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva, Flourish, HTML, CSS

This visual storytelling approach makes complex data accessible. Whether you’re a policymaker or citizen, the chart invites everyone to explore Sri Lanka’s transformation.

2024 Impact Snapshot

In 2024, the UN in Sri Lanka mobilized over USD 80 million across health, education, gender equality, and climate resilience. Read the full report.
