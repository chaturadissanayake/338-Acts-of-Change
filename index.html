338 Acts of Change

Discover how 338 initiatives are driving transformation across Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka's development story involves resilience, growth, and transformation. 338 Acts of Change isn't just a collection of numbers; it reflects a nation's journey, showcasing the impactful work in various communities to shape a better future. Learn more about UN INFO Platform – United Nations in Sri Lanka (2024), the data source for this initiative.

At the heart of this project is a visual chart that breaks down 338 initiatives—each representing a tangible effort to uplift communities, strengthen policy, and build resilience. UNDP Sri Lanka has been a key partner in enabling these transformations.

When I started this journey, I knew the challenge would be turning raw data into a compelling story. The data, drawn from the UN INFO Platform, isn't just about categorizing projects. It's about understanding how each initiative weaves into the larger fabric of Sri Lanka's future.
