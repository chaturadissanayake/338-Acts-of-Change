About Me

I help people understand complex ideas through thoughtful, data-driven storytelling.

I’m a designer focused on communication, data visualization, and public interest design. I work with institutions, nonprofits, and multilateral agencies to shape narratives that are clear, inclusive, and visually engaging. Currently, I collaborate with UN agencies and social impact organizations to build tools and visuals that drive understanding and change.

My journey includes crafting visualizations and reports for development projects, building data dashboards, and creating accessible visuals for diverse audiences. Along the way, I’ve used tools like Illustrator, Flourish, Canva, HTML/CSS, and good old pen and paper.

Beyond the pixels and charts, I care deeply about the ethical side of communication—ensuring people are not only informed but also respected, represented, and empowered through design.
