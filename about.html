What I’ve Done So Far

I’m a designer focused on communication, data visualization, and public interest design. I’ve worked with institutions, nonprofits, and multilateral agencies to shape narratives that are clear, inclusive, and visually engaging.

My journey includes crafting visualizations and reports for development projects, building data dashboards, and creating accessible visuals for diverse audiences. I often use tools like Illustrator, Flourish, Canva, HTML/CSS, and good old pen and paper.

Where I Work Now

Currently, I collaborate with UN agencies and social impact organizations to build tools and visuals that drive understanding and change. I also freelance on the side, especially for projects that promote clarity and social good.

My Approach

Design, to me, is about clarity and care. I care deeply about the ethical side of communication—ensuring people are not only informed but also respected, represented, and empowered through design. Every visualization is a chance to make a message more human and more just.
